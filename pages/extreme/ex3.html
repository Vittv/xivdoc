# Phase 2

    Radical Shift

    • Since we're using the AFK markers, just stand on your marker, as center as possible, while not clipping your teammates and trying to stay inside as many glowy lines on the ground as possible. In theory, if everyone behaves and don't freak out, you should be able to not move at all until it resolves.

    For the remainder of the phase, you can use your eyes and dodge and mit accordingly
