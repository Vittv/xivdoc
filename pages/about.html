About

An attempt to have all FFXIV related end game content resources and guides in one place, while following the Party Finder standard. This website uses personal knowledge as well as content from the entire community. Since it's a mix of information and even if someone made a strat, it doesn't guarantee they didn't have any inspiration or just copied someone else, I will keep all links and credits on this page as an attempt to keep things fair.
