Black Cat

AAC Light-Heavyweight M1 (Savage)

Notes

• True North positions for all of the cleave/clone mechanics

• Bait the role cleaves in the same position of whichever role is baiting first

• DPS will bait the role cleaves first (making cardinals safe)

• From clock spreads supports (T/H) move counter-clockwise to buddy with their DPS

• During Mouser 2 roles will spread from left to right (facing the boss) as Tank > Healer > DPS

• For the tether mechanic (Raining Cats), Melee will take the first tether then Tank will take the remaining with invuln

Quadruple Crossing

(DPS 1st, Same BAIT)

Start in your clock spots. DPS will go into the boss' hitbox first on intercardinals, then Supports will do the same.

This is how the mechanic will be resolved each time it happens, either from the boss or from the clones, always taking positions relative to True North (with exception to tornadoes, whichever role doesn't have a marker on their head will go in to the hitbox).

raidplan →

(the raidplan is splitting roles as range/melee, but PF is splitting by DPS/Support roles instead)

Mouser 2

(THD)

For each cast of Elevate and Eviscerate, player with the marker will take a safe panel to get slammed or thrown, then the party will pre-position on a safe panel (away from marked player) in the order of Tank > Healer > DPS from left to right facing the boss.

This ensures that if the cast is Splintering Nails (role-based cones), everyone will be safe for the hit. If it's party share, party will collapse to the Healers.

Raining Cats

(MTTT)

Group 1 pre-position on the left flank of the boss' hitbox, Group 2 on the right max-melee. This is for the baited wind aoes.

Melee will take the first tethers, then swap with the Tanks who will take the first one with mits, then invuln the rest.
