# Notes

    shabin

    • Follow the pastebin by Shabadadu (uptime alternatives are also included)

    Uptime Sunrise

      (auto-cade sunrise)

    • For gun baits, stand on the direct edge of the marker on the side that is the same direction as towers (N towers = North side of the marker) making sure you are in the very center of the marker.
    • (Note: this strat requires accurate waymarks on the 2nd phase platform)

    Hector

    • The Hector strats are exactly the same as the shabin.

# Witch Hunt

    (HTRM)

  • TL;DR: Range bait far, Melee bait close.
  • -
  • Normal patterns have you dodging in/out on your waymark, and baiting off of your waymark.
  • -
  • The "cursed" pattern is when Melee have to bait close at max-melee, dodging in/out off of your waymark and baiting on your waymark.
  • -
    • https://raidplan.io/plan/LYdEWYHYPA3tr9KT →

# Transition

    (C is new North)

  • Rotate with the boss and dodge in your new relative quadrant with your partner.
  • -
  • If Melee takes the vulnerability hit, Range move forward and cover for the next shared hit.
  • -
    • https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=468692025642271&preview=1 →

# Sunrise Sabbath

    (Uptime / auto-cade / braindead)

  • Note: Both uptime and normal strats use snake prio to determine the gun you are baiting.
  • Gun baits will stand on the very center edge of the waymark in the same direction of the towers.
  • -
  • This requires that the markers have been placed accurately, to avoid clipping. Ensure you have the correct markers (available in the Shabin or below ↓).
  • -
  • Tower soaks just soak towers.
