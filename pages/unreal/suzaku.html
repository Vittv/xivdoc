Suzaku

Hells' Kier (Unreal)

Disclaimer: Unreal fights are the same as their Extreme versions but synced up to the current max level of the expansion. But even if they're the same fight, as the years go by, sometimes, the pf standard strats and resolutions can change. That usually involves ignoring a mechanic by resolving it with Tank LB as well as other methods. Have in mind that in order to unlock these fights, you only ever need to beat its normal mode and have unlocked Faux Hollows in Idyllshire.

Screams of the Damned

raid-wide

Rout

• Suzaku charges at the center (straight-line AoE) and marks everyone with Rekindle (circular AoE)

• Birds on the ground will revive if clipped by Rekindle. Spread to avoid both the charge and birds

Screams of the Damned

raid-wide

Eternal Flame

• 8 arrows will appear, grab a spot

• then face the same direction as the arrow

• orbs will come toward center, facing the correct direction will block it

• if you do it correctly you get a damage up buff (10 total)

The real goal here is to not let the orbs touch the center, as it will feed the boss and make it do more damage after this. By dancing and getting the buffs to ourselves, we not only get stronger, but avoid a catastrophe.

Scarlet Fever

Big transition raid-wide (does more or less damage based on how many orbs were missed)

Phase 2

(big hole on center)

Southron Star

raid-wide

Mesmerizing Melody (Pull) or Ruthless Refrain (Push)

• Mesmerizing Melody pulls everyone towards the center. Move AWAY to avoid falling at the center hole

• Ruthless Refrain pushes everyone away. Stay CLOSE to avoid falling off the edge

• anti-KB works

Well of Flame

• line aoe to random player

• 4 players get aoe'd

• after spreading, party stacks on marked healer at C

Phantom Flurry

• TB that applies a vuln debuff and then a 180 frontal IK aoe

• mit, OT vokes, waits for cleave, then goes north to keep boss in place

Runes

• The arena will be divided by 4 quadrants: blue, black, yellow, purple

• 8 runes representing the quadrants will appear on one side

• a fire bird will also appear and begin moving towards the runes (always CW)

• whichever rune the bird touches will have its corresponding quadrant explode

• there are 2 patterns per set here: 1234 1234 or 1234 4321

• there are 2 types of movements: clockwise explosions or cross (X) explosions

> just check where the bird is going and avoid that color

Mesmerizing M or Ruthless R

pull or push (go out or in)

Close-quarter Crescendo

• everyone gets tethered to an orb in a cardinal

• this orb will then force you to walk towards its direction

• just position properly in order not to fall off the arena

raid-wide

Incandescent Interlude + Ruthless R

• 4 meteors (N, S, E and W) will spawn

• Ruthless Refrain AoE markers on roles (sup or dps)

• boss will cast Ruthless Refrain to knock-back everyone

• IF MARKED WITH AoE can either anti-KB or get knocked to assigned intercard

• IF NOT get knocked to meteors on your assigned cardinals

• tanks can soak meteors even if marked
