The Jade Stoa (Unreal)

  • Disclaimer: Unreal fights are the same as their Extreme versions but synced up to the current max level of the expansion. But even if they're the same fight, as the years go by, sometimes, the pf standard strats and resolutions can change. That usually involves ignoring a mechanic by resolving it with Tank LB as well as other methods. Have in mind that in order to unlock these fights, you only ever need to beat its normal mode and have unlocked Faux Hollows in Idyllshire.

    • # Phase 1

    • Storm Pulse - raid-wide
    • Heavenly Strike - TB

      • State of Shock

      • 1. MT pulls the boss slightly North
      • 2. OT vokes, G1 stacks South
      • 3. MT vokes, G2 stacks North

      Unrelenting Anguish

      • - Dodge orbs and fire & lightning at the end
      • - if marked with orb, don't touch others marked

      Hakutei

      • - add spawn, OT takes it south, party focuses it.
    • Aramata - 2 players get 3x puddles
    • Storm Pulse - raid-wide
    • Distant Clap - party in, OT gets proximity aoe
    • Fire & Lightning - column aoe

      • Roar of Thunder

      • - Burn add to take less damage from raid-wide
      • - Pop orbs before they reach add

    # Free Fall

    • Use your eyes

    # Phase 2

    • Heavenly Strike - TB
    • Hundredfold Havoc - lightning exa dodge

      • State of Shock

      • 1. MT pulls the boss slightly North
      • 2. OT vokes, G1 stacks South
      • 3. MT vokes, G2 stacks North
    • Sweep the Leg - cone aoe, MT baits it in front then goes to back with party

      • Unrelenting Anguish 2

      • 1. Dodge orbs and fire & lightning at the end
      • 2. If marked with orb, don't touch others marked
      • 3. Three players get puddles, drop them on A B C (color coded roles)
    • Fire & Lightning - column aoe
    • Loop - mechs will start looping
    • Hakutei 2 - add spawns again, this time we ignore it and its orbs while focusing boss, OT grabs it
    • Roar of Thunder 2 - at 80% cast use tank lb
    • Hundredfold Havoc 2 - lightning exa dodge + dropping puddles at A B C (color coded roles)

      • State of Shock

      • 1. MT pulls the boss slightly North
      • 2. OT vokes, G1 stacks South
      • 3. MT vokes, G2 stacks North
    • Sweep the Leg 2 - cone aoe, MT baits it in front then goes to back with party
