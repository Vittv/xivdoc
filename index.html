    Introduction

    Final Fantasy XIV is an ever-evolving world filled with deep mechanics, challenging battles, and a vast community-driven culture. Whether you're a new adventurer or a seasoned veteran, XIVDoc is here to provide clear, concise, and reliable information to enhance your endgame experience.

    The recommended way to use this website is to go learn a fight, then click on its links if there is something you don't understand. You are welcome to read through the entire documentation before jumping into a fight, but it might overwhelm you and not be very productive. Better to learn as you go and are able to practice such concepts.

    The content used on this website is a mix of community contributions and personal knowledge, it is hard to tell who owns what specifically, but I will do my best to display my references used to each guide on the About page. There is also a bit more of the goal and reason behind making this entire project on the About page if interested.
