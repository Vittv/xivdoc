Final Fantasy XIV is an ever-evolving world filled with deep mechanics, challenging battles, and a vast community-driven culture. Whether you're a new adventurer or a seasoned veteran, XIVDoc is here to provide clear, concise, and reliable information to enhance your endgame experience.

There are quite a few topics to be learned about this game. But so far we have two sections which we believe are the most important: Fights and Concepts.

The recommended way to use this website is to go learn a fight, then click on its links if there is something you don't understand. You are welcome to read through the entire docs before jumping into a fight, but it might overwhelm you and not be very productive. Better to learn as you go and are able to practice such concepts.

Fights

When it comes to learning a fight or an encounter as some would say, the knowledge required can be very specific to that duty. But as you learn through a few of these, you will eventually realize that their concepts often repeat in other places. This can make learning new encounters a lot easier, since the more you learn, the more predictable future fights can be.

To learn more about these fights, check the Fights page.

Concepts

The goal of the concepts page is to explain the meaning behind several terms we're going to be using across the entire documentation. As well as their different origins, since some are FFXIV-specific, while others are used on most MMORPGs. There might also be really specific slang that the community uses which is valuable to know in order to have a smooth experience in the game.

To learn more about these concepts, check the Concepts page.
