    Welcome to XIVDoc!

    Duty

    The Duty Archive has all required knowledge regarding current endgame content. Here you can find all sorts of information, such as: mechanic explanations, strategies, raidplans, marker presets, prepull setups, guides, video guides as well as some general tips for specific parts of some encounters. Most of the time you will want to use this archive, while Concepts is used for very specific subjects.

    Concepts

    The Concepts Archive is the home to most required yet not always taught knowledge that is often used in guides and groups. Whether it's a slang for a conversation / callout, or a technical term that needs explanation in order to understand an encounter, this archive has the goal to document most words used in the raiding environment. In or outside of FFXIV.

    Introduction

    • Final Fantasy XIV is an ever-evolving world filled with deep mechanics, challenging battles, and a vast community-driven culture. Whether you're a new adventurer or a seasoned veteran, XIVDoc is here to provide clear, concise, and reliable information to enhance your endgame experience.

    • The recommended way to use this website is to go learn a fight, then click on its links if there is something you don't understand. You are welcome to read through the entire docs before jumping into a fight, but it might overwhelm you and not be very productive. Better to learn as you go and are able to practice such concepts.

    • The content used on this website is a mix of community contributions and personal knowledge, it is hard to tell who owns what specifically, but I will do my best to display my references used to each guide on the About page. There is also a bit more of the goal and reason behind making this entire project on the About page if interested.
